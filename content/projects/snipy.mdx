### Abstract

SnipyA, a chrome extension, enhances digital reading by integrating Meta Llama 3.1, enabling AI-driven summaries, explanations, and insights. Powered using [Modus](https://docs.hypermode.com/modus/overview) and [Hypermode's](https://hypermode.com/) infrastructure, it allows users to quickly comprehend complex content through a seamless Cmd + K interface.
